Who I am?

I’m HARU, a 2D artist focused on pixel art for video games. With training in game development and experience in Game Jams and mini-game production, I create characters, animations, backgrounds, and UI-ready assets that are both visually appealing and game-ready.

I care deeply about detail, consistency, and player experience, aiming to deliver art that feels alive and fits perfectly within each project’s world.
